Privacy Policy (You Can Use This As-Is) Privacy Policy Last updated: January 2026 Your privacy matters. This app was designed to support you, not track you. Information We Collect This app does not require an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or location. All data you enter — including urge logs, challenge progress, and usage statistics — is stored locally on your device. How Your Data Is Used Your data is used only to: • Display your progress • Track urges and challenges • Provide insights and visual feedback within the app Your data is never sold, shared, or transmitted to external servers. Deleting the app removes all stored data. Children’s Privacy This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
